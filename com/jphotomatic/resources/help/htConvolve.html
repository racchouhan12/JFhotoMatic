HOW TO CONVOLUTION EFFECTS TO AN IMAGE?
To shear an image just follow the points given below:-
  • Load an image from the FILE menu i.e Import image.
  • Then press Sharpen Effect button of the Convolve tab to give sharpness to the image. PRESS THE BUTTON MORE THAN ONCE TO GET BETTER RESULTS.
    • OR
  • Press Blur Effect button of the Convolve tab to give blur effect. PRESS THE BUTTON MORE THAN ONCE TO GET BETTER RESULTS
    • OR
  • Press Negative Effect button of the Convolve tab to give negative effect to the image.
