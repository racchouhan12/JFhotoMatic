HOW TO GIVE PHOTO EFFECTS TO AN IMAGE?
To give photo effects to an image just follow the points given below:-
  • Load an image from the FILE menu i.e Import image.
  • Then press Black & White Effect button of the Photo Effects tab or select Black & White Effect.. from Edit menu to make the image black & white.
    • OR
  • Press Photo YCC Effect button of the Photo Effects tab or select Photo YCC Effect.. from Edit menu to give photo YCC effect to the image. PRESS THE BUTTON MORE THAN ONCE TO GET BETTER RESULTS
    • OR
  • Press Negative Effect button of the Photo Effects tab or select Negative Effect.. from Edit menu to give negative effects to the image.
